About Us 🏠

Who we are and what we do

Our Story

Paws & Hearts was started in 2020 by a group of animal lovers who wanted to make a difference. We saw too many animals on the streets without food, shelter, or love. So we decided to do something about it!

Today, we are a team of 100+ volunteers and staff members who work every day to rescue animals, find them loving homes, and educate the public about animal welfare.

Our Mission

🎯 Our mission is simple — no animal should suffer alone. We believe every dog, cat, bird, and rabbit deserves a safe and caring home.

✅ Rescue animals from dangerous situations
✅ Provide medical care and rehabilitation
✅ Find loving forever homes
✅ Educate communities about animal care
